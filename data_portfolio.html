STP Marketing Strategy

Project Description

To develope a STP marketing strategy, we pulled the data from ptt, dcard, and Google map using BeautifulSoup to K-means model and did customer clustering. Customer Retention Study

Customer Retention Study for a Sharing Economy Platform

Project Description

We developed two research methodology to look into underlying reason for low retention rate.

The first path aimed to tell the differences between loyal and disloyal customers according to their behavior. The second path tried to identify the main reason for disloyal customers not to use the service again.

Ultimately, we developed a full model which achieved estimated 8% increase in customer retention rate.
